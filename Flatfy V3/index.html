What is SmartWays...?

Smart Ways Laundrette provide solutions to the specific needs of individual houses, hotels, healthcares institutions & tent houses to commercial laundry operations

For the best service we operate material by the follwing steps

Recieve

Shipments are recieved from several zones of state

Count & Verify

Each and every material is counted and weighed for verification and after it a list is prepared

Categorize

Lots are prepared according to their category for washing

Wash

Lots are loaded in automatic machines where experts mix chemicals according to the fabric and wash the material

Dry

After washing process materials are dry in Steam Tumblers and on the ropes

Iron

If Material requires ironing then they ironed by the machines which gives an ulimate finishing

Pack

Material will verified again according to records and then packed in a systematic way

Deliver

Items get delivered to their origin
